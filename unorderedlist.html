SOCIAL MEDIA APPLICATIONS:

Instagram:

  • Feed: Photo and video posts.
  • stories:temporary posts disappear after 24 hours.
  • Reels:short,engaging videos set to music.
  • Explore: discover new content and accounts.
  • Messages:Private messaging with text,photos,videos.

YouTube:

  • Channels:User profile where videos are uploaded.
  • Subscription:Follow channels to get updates on new content.
  • Shorts:short,vertical videos for quick consumption.
  • Live Streaming:Broadcast live video.
  • Monetization:Earn money through ads,memberships,and super chats.

Snapchat:

  • Snaps:Temporary photo and video Messages.
  • Snap map:View friend's location on a snap.
  • Memories:Save snaps and stories for later viewing.
  • Discover:Curated content from publishers and creators.
  • Filters:Augmented reality effects for snaps.

LinkedIn:

  • Profile:Professional resume-like profile.
  • Connections:Network with other professionals.
  • Learning:Access to online courses and professional development.
  • Articles:Publish long-form posts.
  • Job listings:Search and apply for jobs.

Facebook:

  • News Feed: Stream of updates from friends and pages followed.
  • Groups: Communities centered around interests, activities, or affiliations.
  • Marketplace: Platform to buy and sell items locally.
  • Events: Organize and RSVP to events.
  • Messenger: Private messaging with text, voice, and video calls.
  • Pages: Public profiles for businesses, brands, and public figure.
